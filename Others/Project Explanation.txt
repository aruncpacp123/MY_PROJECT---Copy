Online Examination System

Three thype of Users
Student,Teacher,An Office assistant act like admin

Student Have the Option to Register under an Institution or Not under any
If Student is Register Under An Institution He fill the registration form and the admin of the institution should verify that request and confirm that complete his registeration
But if not under any Institution He can login register immediately
Teacher have only one option to login,It is under an Institution
Teacher can Conduct Exam In public and Private mode
If Exam is public any student even the student outside the institution can register for exam and attend it if teacher verify that student and confirm
If exam is Private Only the Students who Is added by Teacher Can Attend It
